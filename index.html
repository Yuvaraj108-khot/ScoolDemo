A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Sri Bhuvanendra Residential School (SBRS) was founded on 01-06-2001 to fulfill the educational needs of children in rural areas. Nestled in 14 acres of lavish greenery at the foothills of the Western Ghats, we provide a serene environment for holistic development.

Guided by the vision of Padmashree Dr. T.M.A. Pai, we blend academic rigour with discipline, culture, and values.

Principal's Message
