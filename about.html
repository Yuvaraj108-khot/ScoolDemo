Our Roots

History & Introduction

Sri Bhuvanendra Residential School (SBRS) was founded to fulfill the educational needs of the children of rural areas who were deprived of suitable educational opportunities. The set goal is to frame, organize, and execute a specific methodology in order to provide quality education under the umbrella of Sri Bhuvanendra College Trust.


Blessed by His Holiness Srimath Bhuvanendra Thirtha Swamiji of Kashi Mutt, and visualized by Padmashree Dr. T.M.A. Pai, the architect of modern Manipal, the school has taken its birth on 01-06-2001 in the midst of lavish and serene nature's greenery overlooking the imposing range of western ghats.

Vision of Founder

Divinity of the saint and sublimity of nature have rendered the campus a balanced blending of an ideal surrounding meant for the all-round development of the young in their formative years. Located just 1 KM from the town, the campus has the advantage of fresh breeze and an unpolluted life necessary for a healthy body and mind.

Padmashree Dr. T. M. A. Pai

Dr. T. M. A. Pai (1898 – 1978), the wizard of Manipal, is the visionary whose farsightedness transformed a barren tiny village into a productive megatown. His message of hope to the poor and deprived resonates in our institution's values.

Infrastructure & Campus

Karkala: "The Wonderland"

Situated in one of the most beautiful outskirts of Karkala, the campus offers an atmosphere quite congenial for studies, away from the buzz of the town. The 14-acre campus includes:

  • Spacious Classrooms
  • Science & Computer Labs
  • Full-fledged Indoor Stadium
  • Open Air Theatre
  • Hostels for Boys and Girls
  • Library with vast collection
