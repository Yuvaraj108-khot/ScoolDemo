Admission Procedure

Admission is open to all eligible candidates irrespective of caste, creed, religion, or region. We follow a merit-based approach to ensure a conducive learning environment for all.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age requirements as per government norms.
  • Submission of previous school transfer certificates (TC).
  • Satisfactory performance in the entrance test (if applicable).

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Transfer Certificate (Original)
  • Marks Card of previous class
  • Passport size photographs (4 nos)
  • Aadhar Card copy
